Stay Alive

by: Artin Kim

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I like the idea of a platforming game. I like the idea of having a moving world. Also having powers ups and coins falling from the sky was a really good idea.

2. What I Learned

I learned that when having the platforms and the background be random there are chances of them being the same color. Which is bad because it makes it impossible to see that platforms. This was the major issue that I got from the feedback.

3. Architecture of My Game

I have a GameObject class that holds the position, dimensions, and speed of the object(because the room is moving). I have the gamepanel class which draws everything. I have an object manager have timers that add the power ups, coins, and missiles to arrays. Then the draw and update methods being called for the arrays. In the game panel I am calling the object manager update and draw.

4. Technical Implementation

I am really proud of the background and the platforms being different colors. I think it really made it so the game was not dull. Also how I made it so they would not be colors that where similar, and the text would also be those colors.

5. Most Challenging Part

The most challenging part was to think of what i wanted the game to be. I did not really know at first, but after a while I just started. The first steps of making the game where the hardest. I just told myself to do it and I did.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

In the future I could fix the jumping bugging out. I could also add a score board that you could save your name next to your score.
